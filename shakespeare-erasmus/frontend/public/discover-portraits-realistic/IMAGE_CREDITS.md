# Discover Yourself Character Portrait Credits

All six portraits are locally bundled, free-to-use, watermark-free images sourced from Wikimedia Commons. Each displayed portrait was cropped/resized to a consistent 4:5 format for the Discover Yourself result card.

- Hamlet — Daniel Maclise, *Portrait of Edmund Kean (1787–1833), Actor, as Hamlet*. Public domain / CC BY 4.0 attribution to National Gallery of Ireland.
- Macbeth — John Jones after George Romney, *Mr. Henderson in the Character of Macbeth*. Public domain image from The Metropolitan Museum of Art via Wikimedia Commons.
- Othello — Théodore Chassériau, *Othello and Desdemona in Venice*. Public domain artwork.
- Juliet — Philip Hermogenes Calderon, *Juliet* (1888). Public domain artwork.
- Puck — Lafayette, New Bond Street, *Kitty Loftus as Puck in A Midsummer Night's Dream*. Public domain photograph/print.
- Prospero — William Maw Egley, *Prospero and Miranda*. Public domain artwork.
